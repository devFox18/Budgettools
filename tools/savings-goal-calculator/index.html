Savings goal tool

Plan your savings goal step by step

Enter your goal, current savings, and planned deposits to see when you can reach the amount you want. Change interest, schedule, or start date and the results update right away.

Calculations follow the schedule you choose. Interest rate and start date are optional—leave them blank for a quick estimate.

Getting started

  • Enter your goal amount, current savings, and how often you add money, then choose timeline or target-date mode.
  • Add an interest rate, deposit schedule, or start date if you need them—the numbers update as you type.
  • Review the results for finish date and totals, then export or copy the report when you’re ready.

Estimates only. Returns are not guaranteed. Everything runs locally in your browser.
