# BudgetTools Site Starter

This folder contains a complete static website for your Budget Calculator.

## Structure
- /index.html        → Landing page with AdSense placeholder and CTA
- /calculator/       → Live calculator app (HTML/CSS/JS)
- /premium.html      → Premium download page (link to Etsy/Gumroad)
- /privacy.html      → Privacy policy
- /terms.html        → Terms of use
- /contact.html      → Contact info
- /assets/styles.css → Global styles

## Deploy (Netlify)
1) Go to https://www.netlify.com → Add new site → Deploy manually.
2) Drag & drop this entire folder.
3) You get a live URL like: https://yourname.netlify.app
4) (Optional) Add your own domain in Netlify → Domains.

## Deploy (Vercel)
1) Go to https://vercel.com → New Project → Import.
2) If you prefer manual, use `vercel deploy` with the Vercel CLI.

## Google AdSense
- After your site is live, apply at https://www.google.com/adsense
- Replace the placeholder script in index.html with your `ca-pub-XXXX` ID.
- Do NOT encourage ad clicks; follow AdSense policies.

## Premium Links
- Edit /premium.html and replace the buttons with your Etsy/Gumroad product URLs.

## Customize
- Colors, typography, copy: /assets/styles.css and /index.html
- Calculator defaults: /calculator/script.js (`DEFAULT_ROWS` list).

Enjoy!
